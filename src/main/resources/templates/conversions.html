Measurement Conversions

Quick Alternatives

  • 1 tablespoon (tbsp.) = 3 teaspoons (tsp.)


  • 1/16 cup = 1 tbsp.


  • 1/8 cup = 2 tbsps.


  • 1/6 cup = 2 tbsps. + 2 tsps.


  • 1/4 cup = 4 tbsps.


  • 1/3 cup = 5 tbsps. + 1 tsps.


  • 3/8 cup = 6 tbsps.


  • 1/2 cup = 8 tbsps.


  • 2/3 cup = 10 tbsps. + 2 tsps.


  • 3/4 cup = 12 tbsps.


  • 1 cup = 48 tsps. or 16 tbsps.


  • 8 fluid ounces (fl oz.) = 1 cup


  • 1 pint (pt.) = 2 cups


  • 1 quart (qt.) = 2 pts.


  • 4 cups = 1 qt.


  • 1 gallon (gal.) = 4 qts.


  • 16 ounces (oz.) = 1 pound (lb.)


  • 1 milliliter (ml.) = 1 cubic centimeter (cc.)


  • 1 inch (in.) = 2.54 centimeters (cm.)


Capacity (U.S. to Metric)

  • 1/5 tsp. = 1 ml.


  • 1 tsp. = 5 ml.


  • 1 tbsp. = 15 ml.


  • 1 fl oz. = 30 ml.


  • 1/5 cup = 47 ml.


  • 1 cup = 237 ml.


  • 2 cups (1 pt.) = 473 ml.


  • 4 cups (1 qt.) = .95 liter (lt.)


  • 4 qts. (1 gal.) = 3.8 lts.




Weight (U.S. to Metric)

  • 1 oz. = 28 grams


  • 1 lb. = 454 grams
